Problems & Solutions

Sound familiar?
GastroCore fixes this.

The most common reasons UK restaurants lose money — and how we solve each one.

❌ Problem 📉

You don't know your real food cost

Recipes priced by gut feeling. No idea if a dish actually makes money after ingredient costs. Margins invisible until end-of-month — too late to act.

✓ GastroCore

Recipe costing calculates your food cost per dish automatically. Set target margins, see exactly where you're losing money, and adjust pricing with confidence.

❌ Problem 📦

Stock runs out without warning

You discover you're out of a key ingredient mid-service. Manual stock counts are done weekly at best. Reorders happen reactively, not proactively.

✓ GastroCore

Real-time stock tracking deducts ingredients as you log production. Low-stock alerts fire by email before you run out — with smart reorder suggestions based on usage history.

❌ Problem 🗑

Waste is costing you more than you think

No record of what's thrown away or why. No visibility into whether it's prep waste, spoilage, or over-production. It just disappears from the fridge.

✓ GastroCore

Log every waste event with reason and cost. Spot patterns — which ingredients waste most, which days, which reasons. Track the financial impact and reduce it over time.

❌ Problem ⚠️

Allergen records aren't EHO-ready

Natasha's Law requires full allergen labelling. Most kitchens still rely on memory, sticky notes, or an outdated spreadsheet that nobody updates.

✓ GastroCore

All 14 UK allergens tracked per recipe. Generate allergen PDFs for front-of-house instantly. Full batch traceability for every ingredient — EHO-ready at any time.

UK Compliance

Built around
Natasha's Law

Since October 2021, Natasha's Law requires all UK food businesses to label every pre-packaged item with a full ingredient list and clear allergen information. Non-compliance risks serious fines — and serious harm. GastroCore makes full compliance straightforward and automatic.

Every recipe you build automatically inherits allergen flags from its ingredients. No manual tracking. No spreadsheets. No risk of missing an update.
